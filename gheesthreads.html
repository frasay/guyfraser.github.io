Clothing for People Who Aren't Quite Right

(But in the Best Possible Way)

A fashion line inspired by disappearing electronics stores, French agriculture, and the beautiful strangeness of being slightly out of sync with reality.

The "Not Quite Right" Collection

Have you ever felt like you're operating on a slightly different frequency than everyone else? Like you're tuned to a channel that picks up signals from another dimension?

This collection celebrates the beautiful misfits, the delightful oddballs, and the gloriously peculiar humans who make the world more interesting.

Each piece is designed to be just a little bit "off" - in the most captivating way possible. Like finding a RadioShack that shouldn't exist, these clothes occupy that fascinating space between familiar and strange.

Browse the Collection

A Note from Ghee

Fashion should be fun, strange, and slightly unsettling - like finding a store that disappears the next day.

I designed these pieces for people who embrace their oddities, who find comfort in discomfort, and who understand that being "not quite right" is actually the highest compliment.

Each garment contains a hidden strawberry motif somewhere - a nod to my French agricultural heritage. Finding it is part of the experience.

"Wear what makes you feel delightfully strange." - Ghee Frasay

Unusual Care Instructions

Washing

Wash inside out during a waxing moon. For best results, hum a tune you can't quite remember while doing laundry.

Drying

Hang dry in a location where you've experienced déjà vu. If unavailable, a regular clothesline will suffice, though colors may appear slightly less vibrant.

Ironing

Iron only if necessary. Some wrinkles are intentional and represent the fabric of spacetime.

Storage

Store alongside items that don't belong together. The juxtaposition keeps the garments happy and prevents them from disappearing like that RadioShack.
